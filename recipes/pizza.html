"Pizza Recipe"

Description

This deep-dish pizza recipe features a unique buttery cornmeal crust. Home cooks will find it more forgiving than its New York counterpart. No need to worry about having the hottest oven or too many toppings â€” just make sure your sauce is very thick and flavorful! I garnished mine with parsley and more Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups warm water
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons fine salt
  • 1/2 cup cornmeal
  • 3 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil, or as needed, divided
  • 4 cups pizza sauce, or to taste

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Transfer dough to a lightly floured work surface
  3. Simmer sauce in pot over low heat until thick, 60 - 90 minutes
  4. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C)
  5. Poke dough to deflate and turn out onto your work surface
  6. Apply fillings and toppings
  7. Bake in the center of preheated oven until crust sounds hollow when tapped
